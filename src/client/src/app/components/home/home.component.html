How to play:

1. To start QUIZ, You must Login or Signup(if a new user)

2. Click on QUIZ LIST

3. Choose a Quiz, Click Start

4. View Quiz, and Click START

5. Now, you are the HOST, and a game pin generates

6. To Play Kahoot, click PLAY link on menu

7. To create your own Kahoot quiz, you must be login and click CREATE QUIZ
